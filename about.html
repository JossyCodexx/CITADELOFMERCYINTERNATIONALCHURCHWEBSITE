CITADEL OF MERCY INTERNATIONAL CHURCH

A Spiritual Church Empowered by the Holy Spirit

At Citadel of Mercy International Church, we believe in the power of the Holy Spirit to transform lives, heal brokenness, and empower believers for greater works. Our church is a beacon of light where God’s glory is revealed daily.

Our Aim and Purpose

The purpose of Citadel of Mercy International Church is rooted in God’s divine will. We are here to raise disciples, spread the gospel of salvation, and live out God’s love in this world. Our mission is to draw all men to Christ and prepare them for eternal life with God.
